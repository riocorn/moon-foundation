Our Story

How MOON Was Born

We were a group of healthcare professionals who witnessed a stark truth: families walking kilometres for basic care, mothers delivering without skilled support, stray animals suffering with no help, and village after village left out of India's development story.

We started with a single health camp in a remote village of Uttar Pradesh, serving 87 patients in one day. That experience ignited something irreversible. Today, MOON Foundation operates across multiple districts with year-round programs.

The moon illuminates the darkest nights — that's exactly what we strive to do for the communities we serve, one camp, one drive, one tree at a time.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services to rural and underserved communities across India, while promoting environmental sustainability and animal welfare.

Vision

A nation where every citizen — regardless of geography, caste, or income — has equal access to quality healthcare, lives in a healthy environment, and coexists compassionately with all living beings.

Values

Compassion first. Transparency always. We uphold dignity, include every voice, and act with sustainability in mind — for people, animals, and the planet we all share.
