Our Story

How MOON Was Born

MOON Foundation — Making Our Own Nation Healthy — was founded by a group of passionate healthcare professionals and social workers who witnessed the stark reality of rural India: families walking kilometres for basic medical care, mothers delivering without skilled support, and stray animals suffering with no care.

We started with a single health camp in a remote village of Uttar Pradesh, serving 87 patients in one day. That experience ignited something irreversible. Today, we operate across multiple districts, running year-round healthcare drives, maternal care programs, animal rescue operations, and tree plantation campaigns.

The moon illuminates the darkest nights — that's exactly what we strive to do for the communities we serve.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services to rural and underserved communities across India, while promoting environmental sustainability and animal welfare.

Vision

A nation where every citizen — regardless of geography, caste, or economic status — has equal access to quality healthcare, lives in a healthy environment, and coexists compassionately with all living beings.

Values

Compassion first. Transparency always. We uphold dignity, include every voice, and act with sustainability in mind — for people, animals, and the planet we all share.
