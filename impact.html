Our Journey

Growth Timeline

2020

Foundation & First Camp

MOON Foundation registered. First free health camp in Rampur Village — 87 patients served in a single day.

2021

Maternal Health Program Launch

Launched dedicated maternal care drives. Partnered with ASHA workers in 12 villages. 200+ mothers reached in Year 1.

2022

Animal Welfare & Tree Programs

Expanded to animal rescue & vaccination. Planted first 500 trees across 5 schools on Independence Day.

2024

Scale-up Across Districts

Reached 84+ villages, 12,000+ patients. 240+ volunteers active. 5,000+ trees with 85% survival rate.

2026

Next Target: 200 Villages

Goal: 200 villages, 25,000 patients, 10,000 trees, and full-district maternal care by end of 2026.
