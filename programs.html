Our Programs

Program 1

Free Healthcare for Remote Villages

In rural India, millions lack access to even basic medical consultation. The nearest hospital can be hours away. MOON Foundation bridges this critical gap by bringing qualified doctors, diagnostics, and medicines directly to villages.

Free general physician consultations and specialist camps (eye, dental, gynaecology)

Blood tests, BP, blood sugar, and cancer screening drives

Free medicine distribution — antibiotics, vitamins, chronic disease management

Ambulance support and referral linkages to district hospitals

Health awareness on hygiene, nutrition, and preventable diseases

Program 2

Safe Births, Healthy Futures

India's maternal mortality rate in rural areas remains alarmingly high. Many deliveries happen without skilled assistance. MOON Foundation's maternal health program ensures no mother faces her most critical moment alone.

Antenatal care camps — monitoring, iron supplements, vaccinations

Safe delivery support with trained midwives and emergency referrals

Postnatal care — mother recovery, newborn health monitoring

Child immunisation — polio, DPT, hepatitis, BCG campaigns

Nutrition support — therapeutic food for malnourished children

1,800+Mothers
600+Safe Deliveries
2,400+Children Vaccinated

Program 3

Compassion for Every Living Being

Millions of stray, injured, and abandoned animals in India live without care. MOON Foundation believes a truly healthy nation treats all living beings with kindness — including our voiceless companions.

24/7 animal rescue helpline and rapid response team

Free veterinary treatment — wounds, infections, fractures, surgeries

Mass vaccination drives — anti-rabies, parvovirus, distemper

Sterilisation (ABC – Animal Birth Control) for humane population management

Shelter, feeding, and adoption programs for rescued animals

320+Rescued
850+Vaccinated
120+Adopted

Program 4

Growing a Greener India

Deforestation, climate change, and air pollution affect the poorest communities most. MOON Foundation's plantation drives don't just plant trees — they build community ownership and long-term ecological resilience.

Mass drives on Independence Day, Earth Day, World Environment Day

School Green Programs — students plant, name, and care for their trees

Native species priority — Neem, Peepal, Mango, Jamun, Mahua for biodiversity

Village community forests — degraded land restored

Tree adoption — donors adopt and track the growth of their sponsored trees
