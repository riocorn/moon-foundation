Rural Healthcare

Free Healthcare for Remote Villages

In rural India, millions lack access to even basic medical consultation. The nearest hospital can be hours away. MOON Foundation bridges this critical gap by bringing qualified doctors, diagnostics, and medicines directly to villages.

✓

Free general physician consultations and specialist camps (eye, dental, gynaecology)

✓

Blood tests, blood pressure, blood sugar, and cancer screening drives

✓

Free medicine distribution — antibiotics, vitamins, chronic disease management

✓

Ambulance support and referral linkages to district hospitals

✓

Health awareness sessions on hygiene, nutrition, and preventable diseases

Maternal & Child Health

Safe Births, Healthy Futures

India's maternal mortality rate in rural areas remains alarmingly high. Many deliveries happen without skilled assistance. MOON Foundation's maternal health program ensures that no mother faces her most critical moment alone.

✓

Antenatal care camps — monitoring, iron supplements, vaccinations

✓

Safe delivery support with trained midwives and emergency referrals

✓

Postnatal care — mother recovery, newborn health monitoring

✓

Child immunisation drives — polio, DPT, hepatitis, BCG

✓

Nutrition support — ready-to-eat therapeutic food for malnourished children

✓

Awareness on breastfeeding, family planning, and menstrual hygiene

Animal Welfare

Compassion for Every Living Being

Millions of stray, injured, and abandoned animals in India live without care or protection. MOON Foundation believes a truly healthy nation is one that treats all its living beings with kindness — including our voiceless companions.

✓

24/7 animal rescue helpline and rapid response team

✓

Free veterinary treatment — wounds, infections, fractures, surgeries

✓

Mass vaccination drives — anti-rabies, parvovirus, distemper

✓

Sterilisation (ABC – Animal Birth Control) to manage stray population humanely

✓

Shelter, feeding, and adoption programs for rescued animals

✓

Community education on animal rights and co-existence

Tree Plantation

Growing a Greener India

Deforestation, climate change, and air pollution affect the poorest communities most. MOON Foundation's plantation drives don't just plant trees — they build community ownership, environmental awareness, and long-term ecological resilience.

✓

Mass plantation drives on Independence Day, Earth Day, and World Environment Day

✓

School Green Programs — students plant, name, and care for their own trees

✓

Native species priority — Neem, Peepal, Mango, Jamun, Mahua for biodiversity

✓

Village community forests — 5+ acres of degraded land restored

✓

Tree adoption — donors adopt and track growth of their sponsored trees

✓

Partnership with government forest departments for large-scale restoration
