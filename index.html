Our Mission

Health is a Right, Not a Privilege

MOON Foundation was founded on the belief that geography and economic circumstance should never determine the quality of healthcare a person receives. We bridge the gap between available resources and those who need them most.

From the remotest villages to urban slums, from stray animals to barren land — we work tirelessly to restore dignity, health, and hope.
