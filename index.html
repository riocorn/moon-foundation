Making

Health is a Right, Not a Privilege

Geography and poverty should never decide the quality of care a person receives. MOON Foundation was born from that conviction — and every camp, every rescue, every tree planted is proof of that belief in action.

From the remotest villages of Uttar Pradesh to urban stray animals, we bridge the gap between need and access with compassion, professionalism, and zero compromise.
